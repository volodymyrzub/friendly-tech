Global Industry Leader Friendly Technologies Expands Its Presence in Asian-Pacific Markets

August 6, 2013

Global Industry Leader Friendly Technologies Expands Its Presence in Asian-Pacific Markets Friendly Technologies will be demonstrating its field-proven device management solution for 4G LTE, FTTH and IPTV services at CommunicAsia2013, booth 1E2-01.

Ramat-Gan Israel (June 18, 2013) - Friendly Technologies, a leading provider of optimized device management solutions for fixed and mobile operators, is focused on the Asian-Pacific market. Friendly Technologies is already deployed in key telcos throughout the region, working directly with numerous companies and reaching others through local partners. With its multi-technology, device management solution supporting TR-069, OMA-DM and SNMP, Friendly Technologies is a key player in the rapid development of advanced infrastructures in countries such as India, Thailand, Vietnam, the Philippines and Australia. Friendly Technologies will be showcased at the upcoming CommunicAsia2013 exhibition at Marina Bay Sands, Singapore from June 18-21.

We are extremely active in this region. Our footprint in APAC is growing rapidly and we intend to focus more of our activities here in the coming year," said Efi Paz, VP of Business Development at Friendly Technologies. "Our deployments and activity in the region cover Tier 1 and Tier 2 companies in India, Thailand, Singapore and Australia. For example, in one country, we are currently developing a 4G National LTE network targeting millions of subscribers; in another, we are involved in a successful deployment with a mix of xDSL and Cable devices focusing on call center optimization for improving customer experience and satisfaction; in a third country, we are responsible for FTTH deployment, for managing triple play services and the home network."
