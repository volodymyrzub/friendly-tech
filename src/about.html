About the Company

Friendly Technologies' device management solutions improve subscribers' quality of experience and streamline the deployment and support of data, VoIP, IPTV & 4G services. Based on the TR-69, OMA-DM, and SNMP standards, the solutions streamline and simplify the entire service life cycle of broadband and mobile devices, from their deployment and on to their on-going management and support.

Established in 1997, Friendly's solutions are already in use by over 250 carriers, ISPs and mobile operators around the world. With European offices in Ukraine and R&D center in Israel, Friendly helps service providers enjoy significant reduction in OPEX, while ensuring customer satisfaction and brand loyalty.

Friendly's product lines allow carriers, xSPs, and mobile operators to reduce the deployment, management, and support costs of data, VoIP, IPTV, Smart Home, and mobile services.
